About Me








For the past 40 years I have been a lifeguard, professional rescue instructor, first aid and CPR instructor. I am certified kayak on the Cheat and New rivers and have never had a violation on any vessel. I have driven sampans in Hong Kong and sailed a Falucka in Egypt on the Nile. I am very familiar with the Great Lakes, the Mississippi in the intercoastal. I was in the first group sent into Long Island by the Red Cross for hurricane Sandy and managed over 200 staff and medical people. I have driven several commercial vessels in the Chicago area. I have a Florida CDL drivers license with over 1,000,000 miles and again, no violations. I have also crewed 100 foot 100-year-old schooners off Maine in the Atlantic and retrieved a satellite for NASA off Chicago per their emergency request.
